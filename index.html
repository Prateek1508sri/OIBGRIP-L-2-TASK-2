MS DHONI





MAHENDRA SINGH DHONI



7 JULY 1981 - Present






"Face the faliure,until the failure fails to face you"
                                            
                                            - MS DHONI
“Dhoni finishes off in style. India lift the world cup after 28 years. The party starts in the dressing room and it’s an Indian captain who has been absolutely magnificent in the night of the final,” are the words no Indian cricket fan will ever forget.The game of cricket has certainly produced many star players but only a few manage to leave behind a legacy that will be unmatched. One of them is Mahendra Singh Dhoni, India’s former ‘Captain Cool’ MS Dhoni is the only captain in the history of cricket to win all ICC trophies. On August 15, 2020, around 6 p when the world was least expecting it, Dhoni announced his retirement from international cricket.
